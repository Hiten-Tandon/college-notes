[[FAIDA/AI|Artificial Intelligence]] is the quality of a computer to do logical analysis and thus imitate intelligence.
[[FAIDA/ML|Machine Learning]] is a subset of AI, which uses data from past and current data to extend it's logical analysis.
[[FAIDA/DL|Deep Learning]] is a subset of ML, which deals with making computers more human like, with more than 3 layers deep neural networks.
[[FAIDA/DS|Data Science]] is the science of analysing, cleaning, inferring data.
